Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about Anunnaki World AI signals, copy trading, pricing, and risk management.

Do I need to give you money to see if it actually works?
No. The Free tier shows every signal we publish with a 24-hour delay — the same data used to compute public win-rate statistics.
Does copy-trading need me to send you my API keys?
Only futures-trading scope is needed, and withdraw permission is never required.
What timeframes does the AI trade?
Primary signal generation runs on 1h with 15m and 4h confirmations. Holding periods are typically 2–48 hours.
How is risk managed?
Four independent layers: macro risk engine, portfolio correlation filter, circuit breaker logic, and per-signal stop loss with optional trailing.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Crypto payments are settled per billing period. There are no recurring card charges.
What happens when the AI is wrong?
It will be wrong regularly. The edge comes from risk-reward geometry, partial take-profits, and constant model feedback from closed trade history.
