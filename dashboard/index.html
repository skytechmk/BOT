Tiers: Start on Free, then upgrade to Plus (53 USDT/mo) or Pro (109 USDT/mo) via the Pricing tab. Payments accepted in USDT, BTC, ETH, SOL, and BNB.

2. Overview Dashboard

The Overview page is your main hub. It shows:

- Live Dot Indicator — green pulsing dot means the bot is actively scanning the market.

- Monitored Pairs — pairs currently approaching signal zones. Real-time price tickers show live Binance prices.

- Signal Feed — latest signals with entry, targets (TP1–TP3), stop-loss, leverage, and SQI quality grade.

- Live PnL — (Pro+) real-time leveraged P&L for each open signal based on current market price.

Free tier delay: Signals are delayed by 15 minutes on the free tier. Upgrade to Plus or Pro for real-time delivery.

3. Signals — How to Read Them

Each signal contains:

LONG BTCUSDT @ $64,500
TP1: $65,200 · TP2: $66,000 · TP3: $67,800
SL: $63,100
Leverage: 7x · SQI: A (78)

Direction: LONG (buy) or SHORT (sell). Follow the direction indicated.

Entry Price: The recommended entry price. Enter at or near this level.

Targets (TP1–TP3): Take-profit levels. TP1 is the most conservative, TP3 is the stretch target. We recommend closing 50% at TP1, 30% at TP2, and letting 20% ride to TP3.

Stop-Loss (SL): The price at which to close the position to limit losses. Always use the SL. Never remove it.

Leverage: The recommended leverage. Higher-quality signals (SQI A/S) get higher leverage. Never exceed the recommended leverage.

SQI Grade: Signal Quality Index from S (best) to D (lowest). Grades A and S are high-conviction trades. Grade D signals are informational only.

9. Copy-Trading PRO

Fully automated trading. When the bot generates a signal, it automatically opens the position on your Binance Futures account.

IMPORTANT SECURITY RULES

- Create a dedicated API key with only Futures Trading permission enabled.

- NEVER enable Withdrawals or Internal Transfer on the API key. The system will reject such keys.

- Restrict the API key by IP if possible (your Binance settings).

- Your keys are encrypted with AES-256 and stored separately from your account.

Setup Steps:

1. Go to Binance → API Management → Create new API key.

2. Enable only "Enable Futures". Leave everything else OFF.

3. Copy the API Key and Secret.

4. Paste them in the Copy-Trade tab → click "Save Settings".

5. Fund your Binance Futures wallet with USDT.

6. Toggle the "Active" switch to start copying signals.

Settings Explained:

- Position Size Mode: Choose "% of Balance" or "Fixed USDT". Fixed USDT uses the exact amount you set per trade.

- Leverage Mode: Automatic = uses signal's recommended leverage (capped at your max). Fixed = always uses your set value. Pair Max = uses the maximum leverage Binance allows for that pair.

- SQI Scaling: When enabled, position size scales with signal quality (higher SQI = bigger size, lower = smaller). Recommended ON.

- TP Mode: Pyramid (recommended) = 50% at TP1, 30% at TP2, 20% at TP3. TP1 Only = closes entire position at TP1. TP1+TP2 = 65% at TP1, 35% at TP2.

- Daily Loss Limit: Stops opening new positions if daily losses exceed this percentage. Default: -5%.

How Execution Works:

- Market order opens the position instantly when a signal fires.

- Stop-loss is placed automatically as a STOP_MARKET order (mark price based).

- Take-profit orders are placed as TAKE_PROFIT_MARKET orders split according to your TP mode.

- All orders use ISOLATED margin mode.

- Position monitor checks every 60 seconds if a position was closed (SL/TP hit) and updates your P&L.

10. Account & Security

- Password: Must be 8+ characters with uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character. No repeating (aaa) or sequential (1234) characters.

- Password Storage: We store only a bcrypt hash. We cannot recover your password. Use the reset link if forgotten.

- API Keys (Copy-Trading): Encrypted with AES-256 (Fernet). Keys are stored encrypted and decrypted only at order execution time. Each user's keys are completely isolated.

- Subscription: Managed via crypto payments. Your tier and expiration are shown in the header. You'll receive reminders before expiry.

11. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often do signals fire?

A: The bot scans 200+ pairs every 10 minutes on the 1-hour timeframe. Typically 1–3 signals per day, depending on market conditions. Quality over quantity.

Q: Can I use the bot on Bybit / OKX / other exchanges?

A: Currently only Binance Futures is supported for copy-trading. You can manually trade the signals on any exchange.

Q: What is the recommended starting capital?

A: Minimum $10 USDT for copy-trading. Recommended $50–$500 for meaningful results. The bot works with any size — positions are scaled to your balance.

Q: Is the bot profitable?

A: Check the Analytics tab for live performance data. Past performance doesn't guarantee future results. Always use proper risk management and never invest more than you can afford to lose.

Q: Can I cancel my subscription?

A: Subscriptions are non-recurring. You pay once for 30 days. Simply don't renew if you want to stop. No auto-charges.

Q: Do you have access to my Binance account?

A: No. The API key you provide has only Futures Trading permission. We cannot withdraw, transfer, or access your funds. Each user's keys are encrypted separately and completely isolated.
